Ethos

Rebel Makers was founded on a very simple, genuine ethos, and it sits at the very <3 of everything we do. We are committed to:

  • empowering people to learn and try new things
  • enabling people to share their skills to help others learn
  • building networks that will lead to new friendships and opportunities

Above all, we hope that our community will feel able to use their experience at Rebel Makers to make a difference - however big or small - to their own life, or to the lives of others.

We are Inclusive

In creating Rebel Makers, Rebel Labs have made a commitment to providing a fun, free and safe environment for everyone to explore technology, in the hope this will lead to positive experiences and opportunities for all.

Rebel Makers is, and will always remain, open and free for everyone. All persons are welcome and encouraged to become Rebels regardless of their age, ability, need, background, culture, religion, gender, sexual orientation or economic circumstance.

Our Rebellion is guided by this inclusive practice, through which we strive to best represent and celebrate the diversity in our society, promoting positive attitudes to our differences and celebrating the uniqueness of our Rebels.
